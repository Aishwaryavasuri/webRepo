Health Importance

The most important thing for a person is health. A good health is ground for all happiness. Our goal is to provide the best care in different phases of life with quality healthcare services round the clock.

Services Provided

  • BP checkup for screening hypertension in elderly people
  • Blood sugar tests for screening diabetes
  • Routine health check-up
  • Immunization
  • Antenatal care
  • Treatment of dog bite
  • Pap smear for screening cervical cancer
  • Preventive and curative care aspects of health

Medical Counselling

Counselling the patient about the importance of physical, mental, and social health, maintaining good communication between the patient and provider. Counselling about major health aspects like cessation of smoking, drinking alcohol, etc. Counselling regarding family planning to couples. Medical counselling covers all aspects of physical, mental, and social health of the patient.
